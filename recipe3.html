Roasted Tofu & Vegetables

The best part of this recipe is how easy it is to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of firm tofu
  • 2 chopped potoatoes
  • 2 cups of chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup of chopped green beans
  • 2 tablespoons of your preferred cooking oil

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Chop up the tofu into 1/2 in pieces.
  3. Chop up the vegetables.
  4. Prepare a pan with tin foil and spread the tofu and vegetables on top of the pan.
  5. Drizzle your cooking oil over the tofu and vegetables.
  6. Sprinkle the garlic salt over the top and place in the oven.
  7. Let the vegetables cook for 35-40 minutes.
