Peach Cobbler Smoothie

This flavorful smoothie is a favorite of mine year round.

Ingredients

  • 2 scoops of your preferred vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup of almond or oatmilk
  • 1 cup of frozen peaches
  • 1/2 cup of frozen bananas
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon

Method

  1. Add the frozen fruit to the bottom of the blender.
  2. Add your preferred plant-based milk to the blender.
  3. Add the protein powder and cinnamon and give it a stir/shake.
  4. Blend the smoothie until it reaches your desired consistency and enjoy!
