Chickpea & Poppyseed Salad

My chickpea & poppyseed salad is an favorite in my house. I enjoy this salad several times per week!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of kale
  • 1/4 cup of chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup of cooked chickpeas
  • 1 tablespoon of nutritional yeast
  • 2 tablespoons of your preferred poppyseed dressing

Method

  1. Chop your kale and place it into your salad bowl.
  2. Add the cooked chickpeas to the bowl.
  3. Sprinkle your walnuts and nutrional yeast on top.
  4. Dress the salad with your poppyseed dressing and enjoy!
