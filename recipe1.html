Avocado Toast

This healthy and hearty breakfast option will help to start your day with healthy fats.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tablespoon of nutritional yeast

Method

  1. Place your slices of whole wheat bread in the toaster.
  2. Use a sharp knife to carefully cut your avocado in half. Remove the pit.
  3. Remove your bread from the toaster and spread the avocado on top of your toast.
  4. Sprinkle the toast with nutritional yeast and enjoy!
